Our Experiment

We didn't want to just research about sprinters, so we decided we would try to make a little sprint competition between Seth, Arjun, Shubh, and Vishav where the 3 ran 50 meters each and we timed it, and we would decide to time it to find our velocity. We are incorporating the equations Vf= Vi + at, Vf^2=Vi^2 + 2ad, a= V/t and other equations
